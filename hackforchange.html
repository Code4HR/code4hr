Inspiration

Adopt-a-Drain

Stormwater maintenance is a major issue in Virginia Beach and throughout the region. It's everyone's responsibility. City of Virginia Beach has the location of every stormwater drain in the city. Virginia Beach also has one of the largest volunteer rosters of any city in the country. Let’s mobilize all of our residents to maintain an active responsibility for our stormwater system.

  1. Draft an operations plan for mobilizing residents, ex., before a storm
  2. Develop a communications and marketing campaign to get people to adopt drains
  3. Work with City of Virginia Beach to promote adoption to volunteers

Mesh the ViBE

The ViBE is Virginia Beach’s arts and creative industries district, a vibrant and revitalized section near the Oceanfront at 17th St. The ViBE is also next door to one of the poorest sections of Virginia Beach.
